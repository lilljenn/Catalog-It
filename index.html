Catalog It!

Welcome to Catalog It! On the advanced page, you can find a full list of MARC bibliographic formats with an interactive design that guides you through the process of each code. On the beginner page, we simplified the process to help students and those who are new to cataloging understand the basic, most commonly used codes for print and nonprint items by reducing the number of codes and explaining more about what each code means. You can also provide us with feedback on your opinions or check out our links page for helpful links for cataloging.
