The following are a list of websites that are free and accessible to everyone, which may be helpful for your cataloging needs.


LOC-MARC bibliographic is a website that lists all of the MARC codes with examples; this list was used in the creation of the Advanced page.

IFLSWEB-Cataloging templates is a website that lists templates with the recommended use of MARC bibliographic data; this list was used to create the Basic page.
